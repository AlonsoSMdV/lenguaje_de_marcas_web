Introduction

Inazuma Eleven is a soccer-themed media franchise created by Level-5. The franchise began with the release of Inazuma Eleven in 2008, with the game series set to sell more than eight million copies worldwide by 2016. Apart from video games, the franchise also has a series of manga and other television series. and animated movies.

First Saga

First Season

    The first season is based on the resurgence of the Raimon high school club, which 40 years ago was the best team in the country, although due to certain events it disappeared. With the arrival of Mark Evans, the new captain, the team will be motivated and aspire to win the Football Frontier

Second Season

    After managing to win the Football Frontier back to the Raimon institute, it will be attacked by aliens (Alius Academy) so Mark must recruit the best players in the country, since some of his friends were injured, and discover what secrets are hidden behind this mysterious academy

Third Season

    The last season of this saga is right after dismantling the plans of the mastermind behind the acts of the Alius Academy (they were not aliens, just some boys who underwent a physical enhancement project), Mark will be recruited for the Selection of players of the Japanese national team. After passing the test and forming the team, Mark and his teammates will have to face a world-class challenge this time.

Saga GO

First Season

    10 years after the historical events of Mark and his friends, Arion Sherwind joins the Raimon High School with the intention of playing football and winning the Football Frontier (FF), but to his surprise that tournament no longer exists but is now the Camino Imperial, a tournament with the intention of regulating football and ending the predominance of the same teams in its final phase, Arion along with other characters will join a revolution to be able to play football freely without any type of regulation.

To know more about the whole season check this blog

Second Season

    After managing to free football from the hands of the Fifth Sector, a futuristic organization (El Dorado) appears, with the intention of destroying football. Arion, unable to let this happen, creates the best team ever (he literally travels through time recruiting players and improving their skills) and challenges El Dorado and the guys from the second phase (this organization is the real antagonist at the end ) through football.

To know more about the whole season check this blog

Third Season

    And as a closing of this saga, Arion is summoned for the selection of players, in this selection only three players (Arion, two of his best teammates) and nine new characters, none of whom play football. After passing the preliminary phase of the FFI (Football Frontier International) tournament, this team is revealed to be participating in an intergalactic tournament with the intention of saving Earth from a black hole.

Saga Ares/Orion

    Season Ares

    In a different timeline after Mark Evans' Raimon was crowned champion of the FF tournament, raimon players are sent to other schools in order to increase their level. It seems that the Raimon club is going to be dissolved, some players from a remote island arrive, these players will be in charge of winning the FF again but facing one of the former members of the rival teams.

To know more about the whole season check this blog

    Season Orion

    After the events of the FF Asuto Inamori (the main character) is combined with a series of 26 players to compete in the FFI (Football Frontier International) this time held in Russia (coincided with the 2018 World Cup in Russia) this time Japan will face powerful rivals like Spain, Italy and Brazil to show which country is better in terms of football. At the same time, behind this tournament, a series of doping experiments are being carried out using players from the tournament.
